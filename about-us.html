Who We Are

Eden Green Developers Ltd is a Kenyan-based real estate services firm dedicated to transforming the way property is developed, managed, and marketed. Since our incorporation in 2016, we have built a reputation for excellence, integrity, and innovation by offering customized real estate solutions to individuals, private sector organizations, and government institutions. With a strong focus on property development, management, sales, and letting, we combine deep market expertise with modern technology to deliver value-driven results. Over the years, our commitment to professionalism and fiscal responsibility has enabled us to foster lasting relationships with our clients, ensuring that every project we undertake is guided by quality, transparency, and the vision of shaping a sustainable real estate future in Kenya and beyond.

Our Presence

Eden Green Developers Ltd operates primarily in Nairobi and its environs, serving a diverse range of clients in both residential and commercial real estate.

We have built a strong presence in key areas such as Kasarani, Ngumba, Roasters, Ruaka, Kileleshwa, Lavington, Kilimani, and Westlands. By leveraging modern technology, we offer landlords and tenants real-time access to property data, ensuring efficient and transparent service delivery.
